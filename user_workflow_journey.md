# User Workflow Journey: Youth Account Fund Management

## Story Overview
**Story ID**: SCIB-25  
**Story**: Parent can allocate and manage funds for a youth account  
**Business Goal**: AS a parent or guardian I WANT to allocate and manage funds for my child's youth account SO THAT I can teach financial responsibility while maintaining visibility and control over spending

---

## Experience Analysis

### Primary User Experience: Youth Account Management
Parents/guardians managing their child's financial education and spending through digital banking tools.

### Key Scenarios Identified:
1. **Account Overview & Monitoring**
2. **Fund Allocation & Transfer**
3. **Spending Limit Management**
4. **Transaction Monitoring & Analysis**
5. **Error Handling & Edge Cases**
